**Headline:** Build an Intelligence-Led Operational Fabric with AiDAP.

**The Overview:**
AiDAP is an enterprise-grade agentic platform designed to create a scalable operating model for core business functions. By combining hierarchical agents, semantic knowledge graphs, and flexible inference models, AiDAP transforms operations from manual, fragmented support tasks into a coordinated, autonomous execution fabric.

**The Complexity Ceiling We Break:**
- **Disconnected Tools:** Unifies fragmented point-solutions into a single agentic workflow.
- **Operational Amnesia:** Ingests millions of pages of enterprise context to ensure agents learn from every outcome.
- **Inflexible Scaling:** Enables volume handling through autonomous agents rather than increased headcount.

**Key Platform Capabilities:**
1. **Hierarchical Agent Model:** Master Agents orchestrate specialized Deep Agents (Finance, Sales, Marketing) dynamically based on the task and context.
2. **Semantic Knowledge Graph:** Grounds AI in true enterprise context—policies, workflows, prior decisions, and support tickets.
3. **Intelligent Inference Layer:** Switches between models to optimize both performance and operating costs (inference switching).
4. **Human-in-the-Loop Governance:** Threshold-based autonomy ensures human experts govern high-risk, ambiguous, or sensitive exceptions.

**Impact Across Domains:**
- **Finance Operations:** Automates invoice validation, coding recommendations, and dispute handling.
- **Sales Operations:** Enhances lead qualification, proposal support, and CRM hygiene.
- **Marketing Operations:** Streamlines campaign orchestration and localizes assets with 100% brand compliance.
- **Back-Office & KPO:** Powers research synthesis, claims review, and high-volume document verification.

AiDAP creates an agentic operating model that transforms how work gets done. It doesn't just automate steps; it understands the business context.

### 1. Hierarchical Agent Orchestration
A **Master Agent** interprets operational goals and orchestrates specialized **Deep Agents** to perform complex tasks—from invoice validation to lead scoring—ensuring the right level of reasoning for every scenario.

### 2. Large-Scale Semantic Memory
Our knowledge graph ingests millions of pages of policies, workflows, and historical data. This grounds every agent in your specific enterprise context, reducing errors and accelerating onboarding.

### 3. Intelligent Inference Layer
AiDAP optimizes performance and cost by intelligently switching between custom models, low-cost models, and advanced reasoning models based on the task's complexity.

### 4. Human-in-the-Loop Governance
We preserve human oversight for high-risk or ambiguous decisions. AiDAP handles the routine, while your specialists govern the exceptions with full contextual visibility.

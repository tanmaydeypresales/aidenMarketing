L1–L3 escalation model
L1
Autonomous execution. Agents clear repetitive event classes — known failures, retries, duplicates, validation breaches — within policy thresholds.
L2
Supervised exceptions. Humans review low-confidence decisions, non-standard remediations, and policy-sensitive events. Context surfaced from the graph.
L3
Engineering only. Platform defects, architectural issues, unknown patterns, design-level changes. L3 stops absorbing L1 work.

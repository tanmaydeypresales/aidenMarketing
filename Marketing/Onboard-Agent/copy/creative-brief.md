# Creative Brief — Aiden AI Onboard Agent

## Objective
Promote the Aiden AI Onboard Agent as the premier enterprise-grade solution for automating the onboarding lifecycle. Position it as an "AI Onboarding Specialist" that reduces ramp-up time, cuts administrative costs, and ensures 100% compliance.

## Audience
- **Primary:** HR Directors, Chief Human Resources Officers (CHROs), People Ops Managers.
- **Secondary:** COOs, IT Decision Makers (for systems integration), Department Heads scaling rapidly.

## Pain Points
- High manual effort in paperwork and data entry.
- Inconsistent onboarding experiences across global offices.
- Long "time-to-productivity" for new hires.
- Compliance risks and missing signatures.
- High volume of FAQ support tickets for HR teams.

## Value Proposition
The Aiden AI Onboard Agent is an agentic AI system that orchestrates the entire onboarding journey—from document collection to personalized training—ensuring a seamless, 24/7 experience for new hires while freeing HR teams to focus on strategy.

## Differentiators
- **Agentic Orchestration:** Not just a chatbot; it proactively manages tasks and system integrations.
- **Enterprise-Scale:** Built on the Aiden AI platform with full governance and security.
- **Straight-Through Fulfillment:** Directly updates HRIS/CRM/Payroll systems.
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** Seamlessly escalates complex issues to human specialists with full context.

## Proof Points (Assumptions)
- Reduces time-to-productivity by 30%+.
- Automates 80% of routine onboarding tasks.
- 24/7 support in 40+ languages.
- Integration with Slack, Teams, Workday, SAP, etc.

## Tone
- Executive, Credible, Outcome-led, Modern, Intelligent.

## Channel/Format
- **Landing Page:** Narrative overview and lead capture.
- **LinkedIn Carousel:** 10-slide visual journey.
- **Executive Email:** Direct outreach for HR leaders.
- **One-Pager:** Print-ready leave-behind.

## CTA
- "Book an Onboarding Strategy Session"
- "Watch the Agent in Action"

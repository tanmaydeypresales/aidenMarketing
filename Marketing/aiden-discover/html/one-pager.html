The Challenge

Enterprise AI initiatives stall on the same problem: the data foundation isn't ready. Knowledge is fragmented across documents, schemas, recordings, legacy systems, Figma files, and live applications — unstructured, disconnected, and inaccessible to AI reasoning. Building a data layer from scratch takes months. Most organizations can't wait.

The Solution

Aiden Discover is Aiden AI's plug-and-play discovery and intelligence foundation. A collection of agents that ingests multi-modal enterprise data, transforms it into a semantic knowledge graph, and makes it immediately usable for AI reasoning, insight generation, and downstream action — without a heavy upfront foundation build. Not a search index. A semantic intelligence foundation.

How It Works
01 Multi-Modal Ingestion Prebuilt connectors ingest documents, recordings, schemas, logs, requirements, test cases, Figma, and live URLs automatically
02 Semantic Knowledge Graph Converts data into a structured graph capturing relationships, dependencies, and business meaning
03 Intelligence & Inference Purpose-built tools generate insights and support reasoning across large-scale enterprise context
04 Agent Orchestration Connects to Aiden's multi-agent framework for migration, testing, deployment, and operations

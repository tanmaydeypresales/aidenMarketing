The Platform

Introducing Aiden Discover.

Aiden Discover is a collection of intelligent agents designed to ingest multi-modal enterprise data and transform it into a semantic knowledge graph — structured, contextual, and immediately usable for AI. Instead of requiring organizations to first build a complex AI data layer, Aiden Discover works on a plug-and-play model with prebuilt connectors and automated ingestion. An intelligence and inference layer makes it available for reasoning, insight generation, and action — in a fraction of the time.

"Not a discovery tool. The intelligence foundation for enterprise AI transformation."

How It Works

Four layers. One connected intelligence foundation.

01

Multi-Modal Ingestion

Prebuilt connectors ingest enterprise data across any format: documents, PDFs, audio/video recordings, database schemas, application logs, requirements artefacts, test cases, Figma design files, and live application URLs. Every source ingested, normalized, and prepared — no manual curation required.

02

Semantic Knowledge Graph

Ingested data is converted into a semantic knowledge graph that captures not just content, but context: relationships between entities, dependencies across systems, business rules embedded in process logic, and meaning that would otherwise be lost in raw data.

03

Intelligence & Inference

Purpose-built tools operate over the semantic layer to generate insights, answer complex queries, support reasoning, and drive decisions across large-scale enterprise context. This is where knowledge becomes intelligence.

04

Agent Orchestration & Action

The foundation connects directly to Aiden's multi-agent framework — enabling discovery to flow seamlessly into modernization, migration, testing, deployment, conversational interfaces, and operational intelligence. Discovery is not the end. It is the beginning.

## SECTION 1: THE PROBLEM

**Headline:** AI projects stall before they start.

**Body:**
The most common reason enterprise AI initiatives fail isn't capability — it's context. AI systems need structured, meaningful, interconnected knowledge to reason and act. Enterprises have that knowledge — but it lives in the wrong places.

- **Fragmented formats** — documents, PDFs, recordings, CAD files, Figma designs, schemas, logs, and live applications — all disconnected
- **No shared context** — systems don't understand relationships, dependencies, or business meaning across data
- **Manual discovery** — teams spend weeks cataloguing legacy systems before a single AI use case can begin
- **Static outputs** — traditional discovery produces stale artefacts that are outdated before they're used

The result: months of upfront data foundation work before any AI value is delivered.

## SECTION 3: HOW IT WORKS

**Headline:** Four layers. One connected intelligence foundation.

---

**01 — MULTI-MODAL INGESTION**
Prebuilt connectors ingest enterprise data across any format: documents, PDFs, audio and video recordings, database schemas, application logs, requirements artefacts, test cases, Figma design files, and live application URLs. Every source is ingested, normalized, and prepared for semantic processing — with no manual curation required.

---

**02 — SEMANTIC KNOWLEDGE GRAPH**
Ingested data is converted into a semantic knowledge graph that captures not just content, but context: relationships between entities, dependencies across systems, business rules embedded in process logic, and meaning that would otherwise be lost in raw data. This makes enterprise knowledge usable by AI — not just searchable.

---

**03 — INTELLIGENCE AND INFERENCE LAYER**
On top of the knowledge graph sits Aiden's inference capability: purpose-built tools that operate over the semantic layer to generate insights, answer complex queries, support reasoning, and drive decisions across large-scale enterprise context. This is where knowledge becomes intelligence.

---

**04 — AGENT ORCHESTRATION AND ACTION**
The foundation connects directly to Aiden's multi-agent framework and broader ecosystem — enabling discovery to flow seamlessly into downstream action: modernization, migration, testing, deployment, conversational interfaces, and operational intelligence. Discovery is not the end. It is the beginning.

## THE CHALLENGE

Enterprise AI initiatives stall on the same problem: the data foundation isn't ready. Knowledge is fragmented across documents, schemas, recordings, legacy systems, Figma files, and live applications — unstructured, disconnected, and inaccessible to AI reasoning. Building a data layer from scratch takes months. Most organizations can't wait.

---

## THE SOLUTION

Aiden Discover is Aiden AI's plug-and-play discovery and intelligence foundation. A collection of agents that ingests multi-modal enterprise data, transforms it into a semantic knowledge graph, and makes it immediately usable for AI reasoning, insight generation, and downstream action — without a heavy upfront foundation build.

**Not a search index. A semantic intelligence foundation.**

---

## HOW IT WORKS

| # | Layer | What It Does |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 01 | **Multi-Modal Ingestion** | Prebuilt connectors ingest documents, recordings, schemas, logs, requirements, test cases, Figma designs, and live URLs automatically |
| 02 | **Semantic Knowledge Graph** | Converts ingested data into a structured knowledge graph capturing relationships, dependencies, and business meaning |
| 03 | **Intelligence & Inference** | Purpose-built tools generate insights, support reasoning, and drive decisions across large-scale enterprise context |
| 04 | **Agent Orchestration** | Connects directly to Aiden's multi-agent framework for migration, testing, deployment, and operational action |

# One-Pager — Autonomous Partner Onboarding

## Headline
**Partner onboarding, as an AI-led pipeline.**

## Sub-headline
AiDAP's Onboarding Agents run the full B2B partner lifecycle — intake, mapping, connectivity, collaboration, and go-live — governed, auditable, and measurably faster every time.

## The problem (3 bullets)
- Onboarding cycles stretch from days to months, one partner at a time
- Spreadsheets, email threads, and tribal knowledge hide the real status
- Every partner's standard variation is treated as a brand-new project

## The AiDAP solution (6 value pillars)

**1. AI-led intake**
Intake Agents read documents, specs, samples, and emails — and generate context-aware questionnaires to close gaps early.

**2. Standards that match reality**
EDI X12, EDIFACT, XML, JSON, CSV, APIs, flat files, and partner-specific variants — understood as reusable knowledge, not tribal memory.

**3. Confidence-scored mapping**
Mapping Agents propose source-to-target transformations with confidence scores; humans review only where it matters.

**4. Connectivity, governed**
AS2, SFTP, HTTPS, REST, SOAP, events — endpoint, credential, and acknowledgment setup runs inside the same governed workflow.

**5. Partner Collaboration Hub**
One auditable portal replaces email threads. Partners, internal teams, and approvers work in the same place.

**6. Measurable pipeline**
Real-time milestones, SLA tracking, and a continuous learning loop that makes every onboarding faster than the last.

## Business outcomes
- Cycle-time compression on repeat partner classes
- First-time-right mapping through template reuse
- Manual coordination hours reduced per onboarding
- Audit-ready governance across every interaction
- Scale without proportional headcount growth

# Creative Brief — Autonomous Partner Onboarding

**Objective**
Generate qualified demand and executive credibility for AiDAP's Autonomous Partner Onboarding offering among enterprises running high-volume B2B partner ecosystems.

**Primary Audience**
- VP / Head of B2B Integration & EDI
- Head of Partner Operations / Supplier Onboarding
- CIO / CTO in insurance, healthcare, banking, logistics, retail, manufacturing
- Director of Supply Chain Technology

**Secondary Audience**
- Enterprise architects evaluating integration and iPaaS modernization
- Procurement and vendor management leaders

**Funnel Stage**
Awareness → Consideration. The reader knows onboarding is painful but does not yet believe it can be autonomous. Copy must move them from skepticism to "we need to see this."

**Pain Points**
- Weeks-to-months onboarding cycles per partner
- Spreadsheet + email chaos with no audit trail
- Tribal knowledge locked in a handful of analysts
- Each partner's standard variation treated as a brand-new project
- Disconnected business and technical onboarding tracks
- No real SLA visibility or throughput metric
- Mapping quality varies by who builds it

**Desired Action**
Book a 30-minute working session with an AiDAP solution architect, or download the one-pager.

**Value Proposition**
Turn partner onboarding into an AI-led operational pipeline. Intake, mapping, connectivity, collaboration, and activation — orchestrated by Onboarding Agents, governed by humans, and measurably faster every time you run it.

**Differentiators**
- AI builder, not an integrator — AiDAP is a platform, not a services project
- Agentic execution, not copilots bolted onto legacy tools
- Works across EDI X12, EDIFACT, XML, JSON, CSV, flat files, APIs, and partner variants
- Confidence-scored mappings with governed human-in-the-loop review
- Partner Collaboration Hub replaces email threads with an auditable portal
- Continuous learning loop — every onboarding makes the next one faster
- Enterprise semantic knowledge graph as the memory layer

**Proof Points (indicative, to be validated with client data)**
- Cycle-time compression on repeat partner classes
- First-time-right mapping improvements through template reuse
- Reduction in manual coordination hours per onboarding
- Full auditability for compliance and risk teams

**Tone**
Executive, credible, engineering-led. No hype. Specific about B2B realities (segments, loops, qualifiers, acknowledgments, AS2/SFTP). Confident that onboarding is solvable at scale, without pretending standards variation doesn't exist.

**Channels**
Landing page, LinkedIn post + carousel, executive email, one-pager leave-behind, blog article, sales enablement.

**Primary CTA**
"Book a 30-minute onboarding walkthrough"

**Secondary CTA**
"Download the one-pager"

**Assumptions**
- Metrics are described as indicative until client data is supplied
- No specific client references used in public copy
- Campaign is global; no region-specific variants in v1

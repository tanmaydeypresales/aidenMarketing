# Autonomous Partner Onboarding — Marketing Campaign

AiDAP's Autonomous Partner Onboarding solution. AI-led intake, mapping, connectivity, and readiness tracking that replaces fragmented, email-driven B2B onboarding with an auditable, self-learning operation.

## Audience
Heads of B2B Operations, EDI/Integration, Supply Chain, Partner Experience, and CIOs in insurance, banking, healthcare, logistics, retail, and manufacturing ecosystems where partner volumes and standards complexity overwhelm legacy onboarding teams.

## Core Message
Partner onboarding is the slowest, most error-prone process in B2B operations — and the one nobody owns end-to-end. AiDAP's Onboarding Agents turn it into a measurable pipeline: AI-led intake, mapping, connectivity, collaboration, and go-live, all governed and learning from every prior partner.

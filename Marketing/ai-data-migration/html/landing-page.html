The Five Phases

Five stages. One intelligent platform.

01

Automated Source Discovery

AI agents analyze legacy data stores, schemas, and relationships automatically — eliminating weeks of manual assessment. Every table, field, constraint, and relationship is catalogued, classified, and prepared for migration design before a human reviews a single line.

02

Context-Aware Target Design

Target schemas are intelligently generated based on business context, historical data patterns, and modernization goals — not guesswork. The result is a future-state data model aligned to how your business actually works, not just how your legacy system was structured.

03

AI-Assisted Mapping & Transformation

Automatically create and optimise mapping rules, transformation logic, and data quality validations. What would take a data engineer days to build manually is generated, reviewed, and refined in hours — with full traceability from source to target.

04

Pipeline Generation & Resilient Execution

Auto-generate migration pipelines with built-in orchestration, batching, and scheduling. Large-volume execution handles retry mechanisms, failure recovery, and performance optimisation — designed to run at enterprise scale without requiring manual intervention at every step.

05

Data Quality, Validation & Governance

Ensure accuracy with continuous validation, reconciliation, and anomaly detection running throughout migration — not just at the end. Every step is tracked, explainable, and compliant. Full audit trails, explainability logs, and governance alignment are built in from the start.

The Challenge

Today's enterprises are ready to move from consuming AI to creating with it. But the tools haven't kept up. Current AI development environments are technically fragmented, opaque in their decision-making, and unable to support the collaborative, governed delivery that enterprise programs require. The result: impressive pilots that never reach production.

The Agentic Development Lifecycle (ADLC) is Aiden AI's unified framework for building, governing, and deploying enterprise applications — from intent to production — on a single pane of glass. Business users, developers, architects, and operators work together, powered by intelligent agents and governed workflows. Not AI-assisted development. Agentic, outcome-driven application engineering.

The Seven Phases
# Phase What Happens
01 Define Intent Capture requirements, workflows, and business outcomes with AI-guided intake. Align before design begins.
02 Design Systems Architect future-state applications with AI-assisted modeling. Collaborative, visual, cross-team design.
03 Build Intelligently Generate, enhance, and orchestrate application components through agents. Traceable output, no black boxes.
04 Review & Govern Enforce standards, compliance, and explainability at the point of creation — every decision logged.
05 Test at Scale Automate functional, regression, and behavioral testing enterprise-wide. AI-driven coverage.
06 Operate & Evolve Monitor and continuously improve in production. Performance data feeds back into the lifecycle.
07 Deploy with Confidence Production-ready delivery with full traceability, rollback controls, and zero-surprise deployments.

## THE CHALLENGE

Today's enterprises are ready to move from consuming AI to creating with it.

But the tools haven't kept up.

Current AI development environments are technically fragmented, opaque in their decision-making, and unable to support the collaborative, governed delivery that enterprise programs require. The result: impressive pilots that never reach production.

---

## THE SOLUTION

**The Agentic Development Lifecycle (ADLC)** is Aiden AI's unified framework for building, governing, and deploying enterprise applications — from intent to production — on a **single intelligent platform**.

A single pane of glass. Business users, developers, architects, and operators. Working together, in one environment, powered by intelligent agents and governed workflows.

Not AI-assisted development. **Agentic, outcome-driven application engineering.**

---

## THE SEVEN PHASES

| # | Phase | What Happens |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 01 | **Define Intent** | Capture requirements, workflows, and business outcomes with AI-guided intake |
| 02 | **Design Systems** | Architect future-state applications with AI-assisted modeling and collaboration |
| 03 | **Build Intelligently** | Generate, enhance, and orchestrate components through intelligent agents |
| 04 | **Review & Govern** | Enforce standards, compliance, and explainability at the point of creation |
| 05 | **Test at Scale** | Automate functional, regression, and behavioral testing enterprise-wide |
| 06 | **Operate & Evolve** | Monitor production applications and feed performance data back into the lifecycle |
| 07 | **Deploy with Confidence** | Production-ready delivery with full traceability and rollback capability |

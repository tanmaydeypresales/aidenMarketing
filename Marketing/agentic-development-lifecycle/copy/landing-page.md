## SECTION 3: THE SEVEN PHASES

**Headline:** Seven stages. One continuous lifecycle.

---

**01 — DEFINE INTENT**
Capture requirements, workflows, and business outcomes with AI-guided intake. Business teams and architects align on scope before a single design decision is made.

---

**02 — DESIGN SYSTEMS**
Architect future-state applications with AI-assisted modeling. Visualize data flows, integration patterns, and system boundaries in a collaborative design environment.

---

**03 — BUILD INTELLIGENTLY**
Generate, enhance, and orchestrate application components through agents. Developers accelerate delivery; business teams stay in the loop. No black-box code generation.

---

**04 — REVIEW & GOVERN**
Enforce standards, compliance, and explainability at the point of creation — not as a post-production audit. Every decision is traceable, every output is governed.

---

**05 — TEST AT SCALE**
Automate functional, regression, and behavioral testing across the full application. AI-driven test coverage reduces manual effort without sacrificing confidence.

---

**06 — OPERATE & EVOLVE**
Monitor, optimize, and continuously improve applications in production. Performance data feeds directly back into the lifecycle — closing the loop automatically.

---

**07 — DEPLOY WITH CONFIDENCE**
Production-ready delivery with full traceability. From development environment to live production with auditability, rollback controls, and zero-surprise deployments.
